E-Commerce stands for electronic commerce, as we all know. E-commerce refers to the purchase, sale, and exchange of goods, services, and information via computers connected to the internet. A person can deal with clients from all over the world via an E-Commerce platform. People use the internet network to purchase and sell goods and services. Credit cards can also be used to make payments. E-commerce has exploded in popularity due to its ease and simplicity.
